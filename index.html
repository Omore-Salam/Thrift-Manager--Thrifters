What We Do

We pride ourselves in persenting you the future of Financial Prudence by offering you the following services:

Save, Grow and Monitor your Digital Assets

Along with other thrifters, you will have full transparency of your finances, as well as how much your other thrift-partners has contributed and who gets to take the contributed money at a given period of time.

Take an interest or a non-interest Loan from your Thrift-Partners

With the full consent of your Thrift-Partners, you shall be able to successfully borrow money from the available funds in your account. But note that if anyone of the Thrifters in your Thrift Community refuses to your lending request, your request shall be promptly denied.

Give an interest or a non-interest Loan to your Thrift-Partners

With your full consent, you shall be able to grant a loan request to any of your Thrift-Partners from the available funds. If at any point in time you feel uncomfortable with the terms of the loan request, you hold the individual veto power to reject the loan request.

How to Get Started

Register as a Thrifter

First you need to register an account with us by either clicking on Start or Join a Thrift. You shall be required to submit some neccessary details about yourself for the safety of other Thrifters.

Select/View your Thrift Conditions

Then Proceed to Select/View the conditions e.g. contribution fee, Frequency of contribution, etc... attached to the Thrift depending on whether you Start/Join the Thrift respectively.

Pick Thrift Collection Number (TCN) Mode

Depending on whether the Thrift Collection Number was set to be choosen automatically or manually, ensure you select/confirm your TCN. If the TCN is set to be automatic, the numbers will be distributed randomly. But if the TCN is selected by the Thrifters manually, the numbering will be then set to be on a first come first serve basis.

Monitor your Thrift till Your Pay-Day and Beyond!

At this point, all you have to do is to monitor your Thrift till it is your turn to collect your Money. And you shall be credited your Money Immediately you place your withdrawal. You also have the option of 'swapping' your TCN with your Thrift-Partners as long as the other Thrifters consent to the 'swap'.
